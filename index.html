Hailing originally from Lithuania, Ovidius is a self-producing singer-songwriter. Creating a blend of folk, indie, art rock his music is a culmination of various influences from Grizzly Bear to Tame Impala and The Beatles.

Since 2017, Ovidius has been creating videos on Youtube, making guitar lessons/tutorials as well as sharing his original songs and covers. Through informative and fun videos he shares not only his guitar playing secrets but also the process of songwriting and producing.

As of January 2022, Ovidius has over 1.5k subscribers on his Youtube channel.
